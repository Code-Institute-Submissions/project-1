About Me

Conductor, Composer and Sound Designer Anthony Bonello studied music in Malta, Italy and the UK. Obtained PhD in Music from the University of Surrey where his research concentrated on aspects of the compositional process. He developed a technique to generate new musical material which is based on set analysis theory by Allen Forte.
